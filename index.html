About

Dark Room is a 3-piece indie/alternative rock band with Middle Eastern and North African roots. Recently formed in Ottawa, ON, the band features Alya on lead vocals, Raph on guitar, and Zanou on drums.

With each member bringing their taste to the mix, they create and play with raw, soulful, and rock-inspired sounds.

As they take inspiration from their distinct personalities and experiences, as well as artists like Arctic Monkeys, Towa Bird, and Briston Maroney, Dark Room’s quickly developing their craft and making a mark in the scene— and just might become your new favorite trio.
